How do you create the State Area layer? 7

1. Creating a Map.

  • Go to charts on the left of the sheet.


  • Drag and drop map onto the middle of the sheet.



2. Drag and drop the Drill-down dimension onto the map.

  • Go back to Master items then drag and drop the drill-down dimension previously created onto the middle map.


  • Add it as a new layer.


  • Select Add as Area Layer.



3. Formatting the Area Layer.

  • You should already be under the options section once you add your area layer.


  • Click on Layer display under options


  • Deselect the "Visible drill-down levels" to select the state option.



  • Deselect the City option so as to set the area layer as the State.


  • Under location. Set location field as Area.



  • Under colors. You can choose to color your map either by:

                    -Dimension

                    - Measure

                    -Expression

                    -Or single color


  • I chose to color By measure and my measure was the Avg(TargetSalesGoal)
