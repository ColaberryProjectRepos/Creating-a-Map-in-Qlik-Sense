How do you create a new sheet? 5

1. Go to App Overview.

  • Once done loading the data, click  "Navigation" on the top left corner of the screen.


  • Click on App overview in order to edit your sheet.

  • Click Create new sheet to create a new sheet and name it.

   

  • Name the new sheet Maps and click on it to edit it.

  • Click on the Edit button on top of the sheet to start editing.


When several fields form a natural hierarchy, it can make sense to create a drill-down group.
