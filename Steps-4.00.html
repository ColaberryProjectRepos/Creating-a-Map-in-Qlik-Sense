How do you pull in QVD files? 4

1. Pull in QVD files.

  • Connect to the Destination folder that contains the QVD files you imported by clicking on "Edit Connection".


  • Locate your C drive and select the Auto Repair Qlik Sense Data file.


  • Click on save to save the selected file.


  • Click on "Select data" under your newly created connection.


2. The tables have to be selected one at a time.

  • Select the the Coordinates table and click on select.


  • Click insert script on the bottom right corner.


  • Repeat the above step and load the rest of the tables which are: JobOrderCost, Referral, RepairOrder, Shop, Status, and US-States-Points.
  • This is how your script should look like once your done loading the tables.


3. Load the Data

  • Once all the files you need have been successfully loaded into the  Data Load Editor, load the data by clicking on the "Load Data" button located on the top right corner of your Qlik Sense Load Editor window.


  • Close the tab once everything is loaded successfully and move on to the next step.



If we were pulling from SQL, you would see all your tables from the Database you connected to.
