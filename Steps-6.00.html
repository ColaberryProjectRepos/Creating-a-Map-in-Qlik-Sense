How do you create the drill-down dimension? 6

1. Creating the drill-down dimension.

  • Go to master items on the left of the sheet.


  • Select Dimensions


  • Select the create new option under the Dimensions


  • When the window pops up, select Drill-Down dimension.


  • Select State and City respectively.


  • Name the Dimension.


  • Then click on Add Dimension and select done.


Maps enable you to view your data geographically. A Qlik Sense map visualization consists of a base map and layers. The base map is the background for the layers of data representations - your dimensions and measures. Multiple base maps are available with Qlik Sense. The layers project your dimensions and measures either by single points or by area, enabling you to see the geography of your data.
