How do you size the point layer? 9

1. Sizing the Point Layer

  • Under size and shape, choose a measure or set an expression to size your points.


  • Choose to size by Avg(TargetSalesGoal) and size your bubble size range to the desired setting.


  • Finally choose the shape of the points. The shapes include:

                -Bubble

                -Triangle

                -Square

                -Pentagon



  • This is how it should look once your done.



The size of the points in your layer can be fixed or you can specify a measure or expression to set the sizes of the different points. You can highlight these differences in values further by coloring by measure.
